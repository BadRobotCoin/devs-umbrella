What is BadRobot?
A digital payment currency that instantly settles payment transactions
Digital assets are fast becoming the primary medium of exchange for global commerce, but their general acceptance is limited by the high cost of validating transactions. The key to unlocking ubiquitous digital payments is to effectively reduce the uncertainty of achieving the transaction objective. These problems are economically solved using public state verification through a distributed convergence mechanism through an open and extensible assurance system. BadRobot is a custody token designed to diversify risk in payment transactions, significantly reducing custody costs for existing counterparty networks.

Our Mission

A decade has passed since Satoshi Nakamoto's original Bitcoin article first appeared. During this period, researchers and developers around the world greatly expanded Satoshi's ideas to create different ways and concepts to update our relationship with money. In recent years, we have seen an explosion of blockchains that aim to resolve the fundamental limitations of the technology presented by Bitcoin and expand the technology's reach to areas far beyond digital currency.
While many of these new systems are barely distinguishable from Bitcoin and each other, the same principles of peer-to-peer computing are at the heart of them all. From another point of view, while there have been notable advances in encryption, consensus protocols, and incentives, little has changed at the network layer.
The peer-to-peer network in virtually all decentralized systems is built to accommodate the immutable communication model: information generated by one node must be transmitted to all nodes in the network. This one-to-many model is radically different from the dominant model seen in traditional client-server (or peer-to-peer) architectures. And it is not surprising that many current peer-to-peer network protocols are not suitable for decentralized systems.
Today's decentralized systems are based on peer-to-peer communication via Gossip protocols. These systems work by having a sender forward a message to its peers, and then the peers recursively forward the message to their peers until the entire network receives the message.
While these protocols have been extensively researched to provide reliable information, they are often inefficient in terms of 1. propagation delay, 2. bandwidth consumption, and 3. network congestion. Also, they have no incentive compatibility: for example, a Bitcoin miner has no incentive to transfer the transactions he receives. In cases like Zcash and Monero, where anonymity and privacy are paramount, Gossip's message forwarding feature also opens the door for attackers to monitor the network to infer IP/network addresses of the message sender, revealing private information.
Our mission is to advance the state of peer-to-peer communication systems to power future blockchain platforms, protocols and applications. We believe that efficient network protocols are essential to achieve decentralized networks on a planetary scale. We believe that privacy and anonymity are fundamental elements that must be integrated into the network layer. By leveraging this technology, we can improve the privacy and performance of all blockchains.
As more apps and platforms adopt BadRobot, network participants enjoy greater incentives. Higher incentives make more nodes participate in the network, resulting in aggregated network effects that lead to better performance and stronger guarantees of anonymity. We believe that a single, well-designed network layer will be essential for greater interoperability and will open the door to entirely new types of applications.
We've assembled a team of world-class experts in distributed systems and networks, but BadRobot is no ordinary company. We believe that the true promise of decentralized systems will be realized when all stakeholders have incentives consistent with the development of the entire ecosystem. BadRobot is a community of developers and experts whose mission is to make blockchain technology powerful and efficient for everyone.
